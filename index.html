About Waterman

Adelaide's original buyer's agency.

Founded in 2003 as the first dedicated buyer's agency in Adelaide, Waterman represents buyers and sellers, never the agent on the other side of the deal.

With more than thirty years in the South Australian market, we research, inspect, value and negotiate every step, so the result lands in your favour. We save you time, money and stress.
